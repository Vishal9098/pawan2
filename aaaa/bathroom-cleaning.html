Complete Bathroom Sanitisation

Bathrooms are the highest bacteria-density rooms in any home, with limescale, mould, soap scum and hard water stains building up beyond what regular cleaning can address. Our service covers every surface — tiles, grout, fixtures, drains, ventilation — using hospital-grade sanitising products.

  • Limescale removal from taps, showers and tiles
  • Grout scrubbing and mould removal
  • WC deep cleaning and sanitisation
  • Shower screen and bath descaling
  • Ventilation fan and extractor cleaning
  • Anti-mould treatment applied after cleaning

Can you remove heavy limescale from taps and showers?
Yes — we use professional-grade acid descalers that dissolve even severe limescale buildup.

Yes — mould is treated with anti-fungal solutions and an anti-mould coating is applied after.
How long does bathroom deep cleaning take?
Typically 1.5–2.5 hours per bathroom.
