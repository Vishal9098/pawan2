Professional Kitchen Deep Clean

Kitchens are the hardest-working rooms in any home, and grease, food residue and bacteria accumulate on every surface. Our service tackles everything from the oven interior and chimney hood filters to tile grout, cabinet interiors and drainage systems, using food-safe professional degreasers.

  • Tile and grout scrubbing and sanitisation
  • Cabinet interior and exterior cleaning
  • Drainage cleaning and sanitisation
  • All food-safe, non-toxic cleaning products used
