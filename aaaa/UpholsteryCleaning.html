Top Tips for Keeping Your Furniture Looking New

  • Vacuum Often: A quick weekly vacuum helps prevent dust buildup.
  • Blot Spills Fast: Dab spills immediately to avoid permanent stains.
  • Flip Cushions: Rotate cushions to prevent uneven wear.
  • Use Fabric Protector: Helps repel stains and makes cleaning easier.
  • Keep Out of Sunlight: Prevent fading by avoiding direct sun.
  • Get It Professionally Cleaned: Deep clean every few months.
  • Add Covers: Great for pets, kids, and easy maintenance.
