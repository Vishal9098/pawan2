Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about our carpet cleaning service.

What carpet types do you clean?
We clean all types — wool, nylon, polyester, jute, sisal, dhurrie, and handmade area rugs.
How long does carpet cleaning take?
Most rooms take 30–60 minutes. Carpets are fully dry within 2–4 hours.
Are your products safe for kids and pets?
Yes — all our solutions are non-toxic, eco-friendly, and completely safe for children and pets.
Can you remove old or set-in stains?
Yes — our pre-treatment and hot extraction process removes most old stains including wine, tea, oil, and ink.
How often should carpets be professionally cleaned?
We recommend every 6–12 months for most homes, more frequently if you have pets or children.
