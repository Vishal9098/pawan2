Why Your Sofa Needs Professional Care

Your sofa is one of the most used pieces of furniture in your home, accumulating dust mites, pet dander, food spills, and bacteria over time. Professional cleaning penetrates deep into fabric fibres, eliminating hidden allergens and restoring the fabric's original texture and colour.

  • Hot water extraction technology for deep cleaning
  • Eco-friendly, fabric-safe cleaning solutions
  • Stain removal — tea, wine, oil, ink & more
  • Odour neutralisation & deodorising treatment
  • Fabric protection coating applied after cleaning
  • Dries within 2–4 hours
